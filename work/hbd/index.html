HBD

Apps • Branding • UX/UI

About HBD

HBD is your mobile Happy Birthday app, designed for people to create and share personalized video recordings for their friends and loved ones. With the assistance of a calendar to remind you of your friend’s birthday, you will never forget a birthday again! Wish a Happy Birthday in a unique way through the crafting of a video to be shared with your friends and family.

View Project

It’s a fun and entertaining app — “Birthdays are meant to be shared”

Our Approach HBD

The entrepreneurs at HBD hired Lounge Lizard to bring the initial concept of making and sending Birthday video messages to life. Lounge Lizard’s creative team took the concept to a whole other level and played a lead role in the development of this new and fun platform. The UI and UX team created a simple and intuitive user experience while promoting social sharing and friend building.
