About Motorola Solutions

Motorola Solutions connects people through technology. Public safety and commercial customers around the world turn to Motorola Solutions innovations when they want highly connected teams that have the information they need throughout their workdays and in the moments that matter most to them.

We created the social network. The users created the new ideas and inventions that landed up in R&D.

Our Approach to Motorola Solutions

Innovation Space is an internal social network created for Motorola for employees to pitch technology ideas, enhance colleagues’ ideas, and connect with employees in offices around the globe. The social network was created by Lounge Lizard from the very beginning stages of ideation, information architecture, visual design, development, to rigorous user testing.
